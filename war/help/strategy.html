What is a strategy?

An investment strategy is a set of objectives designed to provide an investor's asset allocation, buy and sell, and risk and return guidelines for his investment portfolio. Generally, an investment strategy will be designed around investor's individual goals, risk tolerance and future needs for capital. Some investors will prefer to maximize expected returns by investing in risky assets, others will prefer to minimize risk, but most will select a strategy somewhere in between.

In TickerTrek®, a strategy is an investment portfolio, where you invest with virtual cash but using real data. All orders are executed in near real time and performance parameters like Overall Return, Profit Factor and many more are calculated by TickerTrek® to evaluate a strategy. You can create a strategy to learn investing or you can follow good performing strategies by expert investors to know what to buy or Sell.

How do I create a new strategy?

Become a TickerTrek member and then log into the TickerTrek mainpage. Go to “Build Strategy”, complete the form and then “Create Strategy”; your strategy is now ready to be edited in the” My Strategy” tab. To place a new order go to “Enter Order”; you can check the status of your recent orders, current holdings and performance by going to “Recent Orders”, “Portfolio”, and “Summary”, respectively. A short video is available to explain strategy building in more detail.

After strategy building, what’s next?

Once you have created a strategy, the stock trading simulation will begin. Depending on your level of interest in trading and interacting with TickerTrek™, you may alter your strategy to maximize profitability. If you feel satisfied with your strategies performance, you can make it appear in the public strategy listings. The public listings allow any TickerTrekker to view the performance of your strategy and any closed trades but NOT your recent orders or portfolio unless they are subscribed to your strategy. Depending on the value of your strategies, you may charge a fee to subscribers to follow your strategy or you can make the entire strategy public. A follower will receive all the orders made in your strategy in real time and can view your portfolio.

How can I make my Strategy Public?

When you create a strategy, it will be “private” by default. To make your strategy public:
(1) login to TickerTrek
(2) Go to strategy
(3) click on the “Settings” tab
(4) Change the “listing Type” from “private” to “public”.

When you make your highly performing strategy public, you can define the following parameters:
(a) Free Trial Period: A trial duration when your followers can view all your trades free of cost.
(b) Monthly Fee: The amount you charge your followers on a monthly basis to receive all the trades you do for this strategy.

How can I earn from my strategy?

Once your strategy is public, it will be listed in the strategy search results in TickerTrek™; TickerTrek will also highlight top performing strategies. Any one can view the performance and closed trades of your strategy but NOT your recent orders or portfolio unless they are subscribed to your strategy. You decide how much you want to charge to follow your strategy. When someone follows your strategy for the subscription fee that you have set, you earn money by sharing the recent orders and full portfolio. How much you can earn depends on how well your strategy performs.

How to Follow/Unfollow a Strategy?

On the main home screen, go to “Find Strategy”. Available strategies are listed on the left hand side of the page. Click on the “Detail” button by the strategy that you are interested in and you will be able to view the performance of the strategy as well as all closed trades made by the strategy. You may then click on the “Follow” button. If the strategy requires a subscription fee you will have to provide the payment information through a PayPal payment gateway. You will not be charged until the initial trial period has ended.

If you want to “Unfollow” a strategy then click the “Following” button. You can “Unfollow” a strategy anytime you like to. You will not be charged during trial period.

How to find good strategies to follow?

Click on the “Find Strategy” and use our powerful strategy search tool. You can search the best performing or most active strategies by using the “Basic Filter”. Using the advanced filter, you can search for specific performance parameters like Annual Return, Profit Factor, Overall Gain, Free Subscription and many more. Find the strategy that suits your investing needs and then start following.

Is it possible to change the settings of my strategy?

Yes, you can change most of the fields in your strategy settings at any time except for the strategy name or initial balance of the strategy. if it is absolutely necessary to change those fields please contact us through email.
To change your strategy click on the specific strategy and then click on the settings tab. Press the “Save” button after making necessary changes.

NOTE: You can not change the settings of a strategy that has been entered in a contest.
